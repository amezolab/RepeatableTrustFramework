## Repeatable Trust Framework (RTF)

The **Repeatable Trust Framework (RTF)** is a governed document‑intelligence framework designed to help law firms produce defensible first drafts while preserving institutional knowledge and maintaining strict compliance with professional responsibility standards. It is built to operate entirely within a firm’s Microsoft 365 tenant, ensuring data residency, auditability, and trust at every stage of the document lifecycle.

RTF combines structured prompting, versioned legal authority, and rigorous governance to support reliable legal drafting workflows. The framework emphasizes repeatability and control through approval workflows, comprehensive audit trails, and clear separation between drafting, review, and finalization, enabling firms to scale AI‑assisted work without compromising quality or accountability.

Technically, RTF is implemented using Microsoft Power Platform and Azure services, with a defined build sequence that covers tenant preparation, Dataverse schema design, Azure OpenAI and AI Search provisioning, Power Automate orchestration, Canvas App interfaces, Power BI reporting, testing, environment promotion, and IT handoff documentation. This approach ensures the solution is governed, extensible, and repeatable across practice areas and document types.

RTF is designed to integrate cleanly with external legal AI platforms while retaining firm control over prompts, sources, outputs, and approvals, making it a foundation for trustworthy, enterprise‑grade legal AI systems.
